# This file contains information about George Brown College
George Brown College (GBC) is a public college and it is located in Toronto, Ontario. 
The college has 202 programs and almost 34000 students. 
It was created by goverment of Ontario in 1966
Applied Arts and Technology is the popular department at George Brown College and 50% of students study in this department.
GBC has many internal and external awards.
GBC Students get opprotunities to develop their professional skills and be connected with employers. 
GBC offers its students mental health support as well as financial support.
Students can buy health insurance from GBC.
Many international students enrol in the college each year making the college community rich and diverse. 
GBC has many associations/communities to help a student academically like Mobi-help, tutoring servives, and ESL etc.
