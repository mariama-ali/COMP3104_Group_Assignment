We learn Devops for some reasons
DevOps culture brings the cohesion of autonomous teams to fast paced, high-demanding environments.
When applied correctly, it has a set of processes that can:
1.Reducing IT overhead drastically
2.Implement best security practices
3.Remove mundane as well as complex manual tasks
In this course, students will gain hands-on experience by implementing tried, tested and true DevOps techniques. 
The goal is to better prepare professionals entering into the IT field.
